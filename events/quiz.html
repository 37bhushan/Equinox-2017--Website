InQuizitive

Event Description

ROUND 1:Pen and Paper

Participants will be given 30 minutes for the pen and paper round. There will be around 30 question to be solved per team. The questions will be based on logical reasoning, quantitative, GK, vocabulary etc. There will be MCQ as well as NON-MCQ questions.

ROUND 2:Buzzer

Participants will be divided into groups of 4 teams. Each group having one volunteer. From each group of teams, 2 teams will go to next round. There will be around 20 questions asked in this round. The questions will be from movies, sports, history, geography, entertainment, politics etc. The questions can be asked orally or in picture or video or audio format.

ROUND 3: Bidding

Each team will be given a fixed amount of points. Before every question, the team must bid either 10,15 or 20 points. If they answer the questions correctly, then the points will be awarded or else it will be deducted from their tally. The top 5-6 teams will advance to final round.
