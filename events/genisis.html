Project Genesis

Project Genesis

Spark the innovation in you

A team may consist of a maximum of 4 participants. Cross college team are allowed. The Project report should include, Introduction, Block Diagram, Working Principle, Advantage/Disadvantage, Future Scope, Report should be submitted before 16th March 2017 on equinoxv11.0@gmail.com  mentioning the names, Participant-ID, phone numbers and e-mail id s of each participant and the dimensions and space required to exhibit the project. Basic requirement i.e. electric power supply of 230V AC will be made available to participants. Participants are supposed to mention additional requirements if any. The selected candidates should submit 2 sets of hardcopy of their report before the exhibition.

Event Description

Refinement of ideas

There will be two section in project exhibition One section for Engineering students, And one for Diploma students.
The teams registering for the event will be required to send synopsis of their project on equinoxv11.0@gmail.com .
The projects will then be analyzed and refined for the next round.
The teams selected for next round will be informed through SMS and email.

Exhibition

Each team will present their project.
Each team will be given maximum 15 minutes to present their project.
Two winners from each section will be selected.
Judges decision is final and binding.
Venue: Project lab and Multiprocessor lab.
